For a creature who spends 90% of its life hanging upside down, you think they'd be pretty good at it by now, right? Well, apparently not. Quite often, the sloth will mistake his own arm for a tree branch and fall to its death. A terribly tragic fate, indeed. Instructions: The objective of this game is for the player, being a sloth, to catch as many branches as possible. The speed of the falling branches will get faster, and the sloth will become more anxious. To earn a life, and to calm the sloth's nerves, the sloth has the opportunity to catch a falling leaf. There are few leaves, so the sloth must be attentive.
